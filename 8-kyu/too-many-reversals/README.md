<h2><a href=https://www.codewars.com/kata/687de0b45ab74765f516ce3f/train/javascript target="_blank">Too many reversals</a></h2><h3>8 kyu</h3><p>You are an up and coming commentator in the brutal world of pro wrestling. However, you suck at telling who reversed the reversal.</p><p>You will be provided with a string in the format of : "X hit a reversal to Y hit a reversal to X...... hit a reversal to (X/Y)"</p><p>Rules:</p><p>  •The string starts and ends with the respective names of the wrestlers (Not necessary     that the starting and ending names be the same).</p><p>  •Input will always be validated.</p><p>  •X and Y will either be letters or single words.</p><p>Determine and return the victor of the match.</p><p>Be careful. Your job is on the line!</p>
